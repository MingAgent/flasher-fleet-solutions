## TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

### Image Requirements
- **Hero images:** 1920x1080px minimum, 16:9 aspect ratio
- **Card images:** 1200x800px, 3:2 aspect ratio
- **Icons:** SVG format preferred, 64x64px minimum
- **File format:** WebP for production, PNG for editing

### Video Requirements
- **Duration:** 6-8 seconds
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (1080p)
- **Frame rate:** 24fps or 30fps
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264)
- **File size:** Under 5MB for web optimization

### fal.ai Model Recommendations
- **Image Generation:** FLUX 1.1 Pro or Stable Diffusion XL
- **Image-to-Video:** Kling 2.0, LTX Video 2.0, or Google Veo 2
- **Upscaling:** Real-ESRGAN for resolution enhancement
